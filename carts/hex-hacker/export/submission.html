Q: How do you defeat an evil hex? A: Use a hex editor

An evil wizard put a hex on you, trapping you in a tower. But you have something more powerful than magic - code. Hack your way through the clues to escape once and for all.

Controls

  • Arrow Keys - move player, navigate menu
  • X - interact with object, close menu
